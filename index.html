Mister Baby Podcast

Sean and Beryl a.k.a. Mister Baby, team up with guests and invite you to reminisce about great times in their lives. From New York to Shanghai and every else in-between, join us for ex-pat and local tales from everywhere in the world.

Ep #2: Start-ups & Animal Dung w/ Alex Duncan

From a teenage computer nerd, to a degree in Zoology, Alex Duncan tells us how he leveraged his talents and opportunities into several business ventures that have truely run the start-up landscape gamut here in China.

Ep #1: Hamburger Stabbing w/ Tim Neesham

From trouble with street kids in South America, to hillybillies of the Australian Outback, Tim has certainly seen some things, and that was all before he landed here in Shanghai.

Ep #0: The Roadtrip w/ Beryl and Sean

For Episode 0, Beryl and I sat down and just chatted with ourselves as sort of an intro to the podcast. We ( mostly B ) ended up telling the story of our road trip across America, and how it tested our friendship, but inevitably bringing us much closer.
